◆ route_type_t

enum route_type_t

#include <includes/records/route.h>

The kind of transport the route serves.

Enumerator
RT_STREET_LEVEL 

Any light rail or street level system within a metropolitan area.

RT_UNDERGROUND 

Any underground rail system within a metropolitan area.

RT_LONG_RAIL 

Rail. Used for intercity or long-distance travel.

RT_BUS 

Short- and long-distance bus routes.

RT_FERRY 

Short- and long-distance boat service.

RT_CABLE_CAR 

Street-level cable trams where the cable runs beneath the tram.

RT_SUSPENDED 

Cable transport where the cabins, cars or gondolas are hauled above the ground by one or several cables.

RT_FUNICULAR 

Any rail system which uses a cable traction for moving on steep inclines.

RT_NOT_SET
